Meet the team

At Family Law Solutions, we connect our legal expertise with your legal problems to get outcomes for individuals and whanau. With a high value placed on honesty and fairness, we can provide our clients the right advice at the right price.

More than simply Lawyers, a key strength of our firm is that we are all parents and family members from diverse backgrounds. We understand the importance of whanau relationships, along with being a part of our wider communities and helping others. Brigham Nordstrom

Director, LLB

Brigham advises clients on a broad range of family law matters and has considerable experience in the areas of guardianship, parenting, protection and property issues affecting children and adults. He regularly appears in the Family Court on behalf of parties. He is also appointed frequently by the Court to act for subject children involved in proceedings and as Counsel to Assist.

He is of Ngati Hine, Ngapuhi, Ngati Kahungunu and pakeha descent, was born and raised in Waikato and admitted as a Barrister and Solicitor in 2009. After spending five years at an eminent Hamilton law firm, he established Family Law Solutions and has been a firm Director since 2014.

Outside of work he enjoys spending time with his whanau and friends, being involved in his cultural, school, church and sporting communities and supporting his wife and children with their interests and events.
