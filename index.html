Meet the team

Brigham Nordstrom

Director, LLB

Brigham has advised many clients on a broad range of family law matters and has considerable experience in the areas of guardianship, parenting, protection and property issues affecting children and adults. He is instructed as Counsel for both privately retained and legally aided clients in Family Court litigation. He is married with four children.

Born and raised in the Waikato and graduating with a (LLB) Bachelor of Laws degree. Brigham was admitted to the bar in 2009. He spent several years working for a prominent Hamilton law firm. Brigham established his own practice in 2014 as a Barrister and Solicitor. He is the Director of Family Law Solutions Ltd.

Joseph Cammock

LLB

Joseph obtained his LLB from the University of Waikato, and was admitted as a barrister and solicitor in December 2009, Joseph worked at a rural law firm in Te Kuiti for over 10 years before joining Family Law Solutions in 2020.

Joseph has a vast amount of experience in Family Court matters including parenting orders, protection orders, guardianship disputes, Oranga Tamariki proceedings and relationship property. Joseph acts for private and legal aid clients, and has been appointed as lawyer for child by the Family Court.

Sharandeep Shergill

LLB

After completing her LLB at the University of Waikato and being admitted to the bar in October 2017, Sharandeep embarked on her family law career in South Auckland before returning to her hometown of Hamilton in 2018. Since then, she has been practicing law in the Hamilton region. In May 2022, Sharandeep joined Family Law Solutions, where she provides legal aid services as a lead provider.

With her proficiency in English, Punjabi, and Hindi, Sharandeep brings cultural competence to her practice, which is evident in her enjoyment of assisting clients from diverse backgrounds with matters such as the Care of Children Act, Family Violence, Oranga Tamariki, Paternity, and Dissolution of Marriage.
